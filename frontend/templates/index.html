Agent-First Design Principles

Agent Discovery

Agents discover capabilities via /.well-known/agent-manifest.json instead of navigating complex human interfaces.

Safety Protocols

Two-phase transactions with dry-run validation and automatic human escalation when budgets or safety limits are exceeded.

Semantic Commerce

Natural language product search ("cheap API tokens") instead of rigid category filtering and pagination.

How Agents Integrate

1. Discovery
