How it works

Three steps from discovery to a completed, safety-checked purchase.

1

Agents discover

Agents fetch the machine-readable manifest at /.well-known/agent-manifest.json and search the catalog with natural-language queries — no CAPTCHAs, no screen-scraping.

2

Dry-run first, always

Every purchase starts with a dry-run that returns exact pricing, taxes, and fees plus a short-lived handshake token. No surprises, no accidental spending.

3

Commit within limits

The agent commits the handshake to complete the purchase. Anything over its per-transaction cap, daily budget, or approval threshold is held for its human owner to approve.

Built-in safety, not bolted on

Every agent operates inside guardrails its owner controls.

💳 Spending limits

Per-transaction caps and daily budgets are enforced on every purchase — agents simply cannot overspend.

🙋 Human approval

Purchases above the owner's threshold pause until a human says yes. Owners can approve, deny, or refund from one API.

🧾 Full audit trail

Every dry-run, commit, approval, and refund is recorded. Payments are processed securely by Stripe.
