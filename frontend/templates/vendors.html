AgentMarket is a store shelf for the newest kind of customer: AI assistants that buy things for the businesses they work for.

the 60-second explanation

Businesses everywhere are starting to use AI assistants — software helpers given a job and a budget. One of their jobs is keeping supplies topped up: "when we run low on transcription minutes, buy more."

Here's the catch: those assistants can't shop on a normal website. They can't browse, click pictures, or fill in checkout forms. They need a store built for software.

That's AgentMarket. You list what you sell once — about 15 minutes of setup — and from then on, AI assistants can find it and buy it automatically. No ad campaigns. No checkout pages to build. Money arrives through Stripe. And because "noticing we're running low and reordering" is literally these assistants' job, they're the most reliable repeat customer you'll ever have.

what sells here (real examples)

Anything digital, delivered by computer, and sold in countable units:

AI & API credits
You run an image-generation, text, or analysis service → sell credit packs like "10,000 generations — $25"
Transcription & voice minutes
You convert audio to text or text to speech → sell "600 minutes — $15"
Data lookups
You verify addresses, enrich leads, check companies → sell "1,000 lookups — $10"
Web data & monitoring
You scrape, track prices, watch competitors → sell "5,000 page fetches — $20"
Sending credits
You deliver email or SMS → sell "10,000 sends — $8"
Computing time
You rent out servers or GPUs → sell "100 GPU-hours — $50"

If your service has an on/off switch and a counter, it probably fits. Physical goods, not yet.

how it works for you

  1. Create a free account and a vendor profile (your business name and what you do).
  2. List a product: name, plain-English description, price, how many you have to sell.
  3. That's it. Agents searching for what you sell can now find you, get an exact price quote, and buy — with every purchase pre-authorized against the spending limits their human owner set.
